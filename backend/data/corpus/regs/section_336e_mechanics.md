Regulation 1.336-2 is the decisive authority for whether a section 336(e) election is available, how the election is made, and what old-target consequences follow. It turns seller identity, target profile, qualified-stock-disposition status, protective election mechanics, and the required election statement into real gating facts. It also makes clear that minority-holder and retained-stock consequences may leave the path weaker or more complex than a simple “stock plus step-up” summary suggests.
