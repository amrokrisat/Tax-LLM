Regulation 1.338-1 provides the threshold operational rules for whether a Section 338 election can actually happen. For this wedge, it is one of the most valuable gating authorities because it moves the analysis from abstract step-up economics to election availability. If the buyer does not have a qualified stock purchase or the transaction does not fit the election mechanics, the deemed asset path is not a real recommendation, no matter how attractive the step-up may look in a model.
