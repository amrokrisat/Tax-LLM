Regulation 1.752-1 provides the operative liability-allocation framework for section 752. It matters whenever a partnership path is being justified partly on the basis of how debt or debt relief will be shared among the parties.

For structure comparison, these rules help determine whether the modeled outside-basis and cash-distribution economics are actually supportable once liabilities are allocated under the regulations rather than under a rough commercial intuition.
