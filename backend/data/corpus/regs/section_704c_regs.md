Regulation 1.704-3 supplies the operative rules for allocating built-in gain and loss in contributed property. It matters when a structure comparison uses a partnership path and assumes contributed-property economics can be shared freely after contribution.

For transactional modeling, this regulation sharpens whether a partnership alternative really delivers the desired economic sharing and tax burden allocation once post-contribution allocations are applied.
