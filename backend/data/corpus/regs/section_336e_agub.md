Regulation 1.336-4 matters because it translates a section 336(e) election into buyer-side basis economics through AGUB and the deemed acquisition rules. It is the authority that turns a stock-form acquisition into a serious step-up candidate or exposes the path as weaker than a direct asset purchase once allocation mechanics, new-target basis, and the actual price paid for the stock are modeled carefully.
