Regulation 1.338(h)(10)-1 is the decisive authority when the parties are evaluating a joint election path rather than a generic section 338 idea. It is the rule that turns seller identity, consent, old-target/new-target consequences, and procedural mechanics into gating facts that can make the path strong, weak, or unavailable.
