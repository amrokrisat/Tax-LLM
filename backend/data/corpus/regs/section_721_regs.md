Regulation 1.721-1 applies section 721 to real contribution transactions and helps distinguish contributed property, partnership interests, and related transfer mechanics in a live deal. It matters when the structure depends on a partnership contribution path rather than on a corporate contribution or a straightforward taxable sale.

In acquisition planning, this regulation is useful because it keeps the partnership path grounded in actual contribution mechanics instead of treating any LLC rollover as automatically tax-deferred.
