Regulation 1.336-3 matters because a section 336(e) election is only decision-useful if the parties understand the aggregate deemed asset disposition price and the seller-side or old-target tax consequences that flow from it. It ties the deemed sale to an allocation framework modeled on the section 338 rules, making seller-side gain profile, allocation tension, and downstream reporting consequences central rather than incidental.
