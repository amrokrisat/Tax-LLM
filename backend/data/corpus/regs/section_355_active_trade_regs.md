Regulation 1.355-3 is the operative active-trade-or-business authority for section 355. It matters whenever the planned separation depends on whether each relevant corporation conducts a qualifying business, whether that business has the required history, and whether the parties are isolating a real line of business rather than merely shuffling assets before a sale.

In live structuring analysis, this regulation often determines whether a divisive path is genuinely viable. If the distributing and controlled corporations do not each satisfy the active-trade-or-business framework, the separation may not be a serious section 355 path at all.
