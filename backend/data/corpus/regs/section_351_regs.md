Treas. Reg. Section 1.351-1 addresses the operational application of Section 351, including the control requirement and the treatment of stock and securities received in the exchange. It is useful when a planned rollover or pre-closing contribution is intended to be tax-deferred but the surrounding arrangements make the control or exchange analysis sensitive.

The regulation is especially relevant when contribution structures are used as components of a broader acquisition. It helps frame whether a rollover really stands as a qualifying contribution step or whether it is better analyzed as part of a taxable sale or another integrated transaction.
