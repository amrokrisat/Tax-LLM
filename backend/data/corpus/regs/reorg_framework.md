Regulation 1.368-1 is the core framework authority for determining whether a proposed acquisitive structure is being tested under real reorganization doctrine or only under a broad intuition about continuity. It directly governs business purpose and plan-of-reorganization considerations that often separate a plausible reorganization path from a merely aspirational one.
