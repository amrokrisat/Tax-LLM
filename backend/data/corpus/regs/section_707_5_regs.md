Regulation 1.707-5 is the debt-financed-distribution rule set that often determines whether a purported partnership rollover behaves more like a sale. It matters whenever debt is pushed into a partnership structure and cash or debt relief is moving around in a way that may be part of the acquisition economics.

In transactional analysis, this regulation is critical because it connects partnership structure planning to financing reality. A path that looks tax-efficient before liability and distribution mechanics are modeled can become a disguised sale once section 707 debt-financed distribution rules are applied.
