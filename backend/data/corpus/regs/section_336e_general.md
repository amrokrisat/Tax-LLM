Regulation 1.336-1 is the principal gating source for whether section 336(e) is even in play. It defines seller, purchaser, target, qualified stock disposition, S corporation target, and the overlap rules with qualified stock purchase concepts. It is the first authority to consult when the question is whether a stock-form transaction can realistically use section 336(e) rather than whether a deemed asset result would simply be tax-efficient if it were available.
