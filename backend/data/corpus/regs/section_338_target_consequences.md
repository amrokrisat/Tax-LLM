Regulation 1.338-2 is central to the seller-side and target-level consequence analysis because it governs the deemed sale by old target. It matters whenever the buyer wants a section 338 election but the real question is who bears the resulting tax cost and whether that tax burden overwhelms the value of buyer-side basis step-up.
