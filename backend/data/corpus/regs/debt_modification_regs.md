Regulation 1.1001-3 is relevant to the current wedge only where post-closing refinancing or debt amendments materially affect the stock-versus-asset comparison. It does not drive the structure question by itself, but it can change the modeled economics if the selected path depends on refinancing, seller notes, or significant debt amendments shortly after closing. That makes it a directly relevant financing overlay rather than a general financing authority.
