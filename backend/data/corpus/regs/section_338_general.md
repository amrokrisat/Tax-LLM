Regulation 1.338-1 is the principal gating source for whether a stock acquisition can support any section 338 election at all. It operationalizes qualified-stock-purchase status, the purchasing-corporation requirements, and the timing framework that determines whether later basis and consequence analysis is real or merely hypothetical.
