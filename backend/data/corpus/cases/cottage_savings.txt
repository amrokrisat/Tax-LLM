The case discusses the realization standard for materially different properties and can inform exchange and restructuring analysis around whether a change in property rights produces taxable realization. While not a direct M&A roadmap authority, it remains useful background support where a structure comparison turns on whether changes in legal rights and instruments create realization consequences.
