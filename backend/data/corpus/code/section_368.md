Section 368 supplies the statutory reorganization framework that determines whether a merger or rollover-sensitive path is even in the running. For this product, it is the starting point for distinguishing a taxable stock acquisition from a qualifying acquisitive reorganization, including triangular merger forms and the stock-continuity features that support or undermine those paths.
