Section 354 matters when acquisitive structures depend on exchanging qualifying stock or securities without immediate gain recognition. In this wedge, it sharpens the analysis of rollover value, continuity-sensitive treatment, and whether the retained instrument looks enough like stock or securities to support a reorganization path rather than a taxable acquisition.
