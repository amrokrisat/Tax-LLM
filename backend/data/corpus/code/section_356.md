Section 356 governs how gain is recognized when a reorganization exchange includes boot or other nonqualifying consideration. It is critical to structure comparison because many real merger paths include mixed cash and equity consideration, and the tax cost of that boot can materially change whether a continuity-sensitive path remains preferable to a taxable stock or asset structure.
