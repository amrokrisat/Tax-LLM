Section 1060 governs purchase-price allocation in applicable asset acquisitions and therefore directly affects whether a basis-step-up story is economically real. In a deemed asset path, its overlap with AGUB and ADSP mechanics is central to buyer-side basis modeling and seller-side allocation disputes.
