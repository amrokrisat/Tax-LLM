Section 704(c) governs how built-in gain or loss in contributed property is allocated after contribution to a partnership. It matters when a structure uses a partnership vehicle to accommodate rollover, joint-venture, or drop-in economics, because the tax burden and benefit of contributed property do not disappear simply because the property moves into partnership form.

In structure analysis, section 704(c) helps distinguish a truly workable partnership alternative from one that only looks attractive before post-contribution allocations and built-in gain tracking are modeled.
