Section 338 is the governing statutory framework for converting certain stock acquisitions into deemed asset transactions. At the rule level, it matters because it ties election availability to a qualified stock purchase, distinguishes old target from new target consequences, separates section 338(g) from section 338(h)(10), and determines whether buyer-side basis results justify the resulting seller-side or target-level tax cost.
