Section 752 governs how liabilities are allocated among partners and how those allocations affect outside basis. It matters whenever a partnership structure is being used in an acquisition setting and debt placement or debt relief is part of the value proposition.

For transactional work, section 752 often determines whether a purported rollover or contribution keeps its intended tax posture once debt assumptions, debt-financed distributions, and basis consequences are taken seriously.
