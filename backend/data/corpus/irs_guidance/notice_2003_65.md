Notice 2003-65 is useful when the stock path is being valued partly on the expected usefulness of target attributes after an ownership change. It adds practical detail to the attribute-preservation overlap by addressing recognized built-in gain and loss approaches under Section 382. In the wedge, it helps distinguish between a stock deal that preserves meaningful tax value and one where the expected attribute upside is thinner than the headline numbers suggest.
