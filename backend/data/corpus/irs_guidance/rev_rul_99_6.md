Revenue Ruling 99-6 is a practical IRS source for classifying certain LLC acquisition structures as either asset acquisitions or equity-style transactions. It is useful in this wedge because it helps prevent a superficial stock-versus-asset comparison from ignoring entity-classification consequences. Where the target or an acquisition vehicle is an LLC or disregarded entity, this ruling can materially change how buyer basis, seller gain, and election alternatives should be modeled.
