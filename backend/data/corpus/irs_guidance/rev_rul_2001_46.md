Revenue Ruling 2001-46 helps where the stock-versus-asset wedge overlaps with merger sequencing and step integration. It is useful when the parties are comparing a taxable stock path against a merger path that includes rollover equity or integrated steps. The ruling does not replace the Code and regulations, but it gives practical IRS guidance on how linked steps can still be respected as part of a reorganization-sensitive structure.
