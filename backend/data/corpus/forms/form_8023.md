Form 8023 is a practical election-mechanics source for the stock-versus-deemed-asset wedge. It matters because it forces the analysis to ask whether the transaction is truly capable of supporting a Section 338 election and whether the parties are prepared to make and document that choice on time. In the workflow, it is a useful operational check on whether a deemed asset path is real rather than merely a conceptual alternative.
