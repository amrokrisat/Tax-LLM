Form 8594 is the reporting form that operationalizes the Section 1060 allocation work. It is not the legal rule itself, but it is highly useful for this wedge because it forces the buyer and seller to commit to a reported allocation and exposes whether the modeled basis step-up is supported by a coherent residual-method story. That makes it a practical bridge between structure selection and actual reporting discipline.
