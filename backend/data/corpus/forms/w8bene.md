The W-8BEN-E instructions help validate payee classification and documentation for withholding analysis, but they are not a substitute for substantive statutory authority. They are still operationally important in acquisitions involving foreign sellers, lenders, or intermediate entities because documentation failures can affect closing mechanics and escrow positions even when the underlying substantive analysis is sound.
